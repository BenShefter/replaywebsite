Our Story

The Problem

As a junior in high school and having played travel sports my whole life, I have a clear vision of what is needed to be equipped to play each sport at all levels. I understand the sacrifices families make to outfit their children to be safe playing the sport they love. I saw an opportunity to make a difference in the lives of many athletes. My goal was not just about scoring on the ice during my hockey games or helping get a win for my team, but it is about scoring in the hearts of those in need and making their athletic dreams a possibility, even if it is in one piece of equipment at a time. By facilitating the donation and repurposing of equipment, my platform becomes a catalyst for positive change one donation at a time.

Our Purpose

At Replay, we believe in the transformative power of helping others, giving back, and fulfilling a need all in one concept. Our commitment is unwavering, and our journey is marked by impactful initiatives and community support.
